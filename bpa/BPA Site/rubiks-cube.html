| How to Make a Custom Stickered Rubik's Cube

| Materials

  • ❱ Rubik's Cube
  • ❱ Scissors
  • ❱ Sticker paper
  • ❱ Printer
  • ❱ Ruler
  • ❱ Images to use

You can buy any of these items on Amazon, you can also buy sticker paper, scissors, and a ruler at Michaels.

| STEP 1 : Prepare Your Pictures

First off, find the image(s) you would like to place on the cube and add the to a word document. Use the cropping tool to make them perfect squares. Then grab your ruler and measure how wide your Rubik's Cube is. Make your images a little bit smaller than that because if you look at your Rubik's Cube, the stickers are a little smaller than the cubes.

| STEP 2 : Re-Stickering

Once you have six square pictures ready, print them out onto your sticker paper and use a ruler to mark them into thirds and cut them but keep the backing on. Then use a thin object like a knife or your finger nails to carefully remove the stickers from your cube. If there is any residue left on the cube, wash it off and let the cube dry. Now you can put your stickers onto your cube. Now you're done! If you don't yet know how to solve a Rubik's Cube and would like to learn you can check out my other site here.
