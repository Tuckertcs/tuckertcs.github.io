| How to Make a Phantom Rubik's Cube

| Materials

  • ❱ Stickerless Rubik's Cube (make sure to select 'stickerless (bright)' or 'stickerless (pink)'!)
  • ❱ Black or White Stickers (make sure to select black stickers!)
  • ❱ Sticker Remover or Xacto Knife

You can buy a stickerless cube on Amazon but it might be better to buy from TheCubicle because they will also sell stickers to fit specific cubes.

| STEP 1 : Applying The Stickers

There really isn't much to say, remove the stickers from the sheets and apply them to the cube. If they come with transfer paper, then this might make it a little easier. Once you apply the stickers you will have a phantom cube which works just like a normal cube but is a little harder to see so it might slow you down.
